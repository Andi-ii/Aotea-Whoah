Recommended Activities To Do

  • Activity 1 : Waitomo Caves

    In Waitomo you will explore the ancient and amazing caves with many different cave formations including Stalactites, Stalagmites and Lime Columns before descending even further underground for your Glowworm experience.Book your Glowworm boat for a cruise on the grotto river with thousands of Glowworms above you.

  • Activity 2: Goat Island Marine Reserve

    So much more than a picnic at the beach, your visit to New Zealand's first and most accessible marine reserve,seeing and swimming with the fishes, is unforgettable.

  • Activity 3: Auckland Stardome

    A Planetarium show to be enjoyed by all ages that explores the darker of the universe and explorations beyond our immediate solar system. There are also interactive displays, collections of meteorites and moon rocks plus telescope viewing sessions. Located in One Tree Domain

  • Activity 4: Auckland Art Gallery

    Auckland Art Gallery is a cultural leader in Aotearoa New Zealand. The Gallery is the arts and learning precinct of Auckland's central city; provide visitors with fresh ways to think about art and participate in creativity.

  • Activity 5: Waiheke Island

    Wild vineyards, pristine beaches, and great cafes; this is a great day activity for young and old. Just a short ferry ride away from Auckland CBD, there are a range of activites you can do including hiking, horse riding, ziplining and more!
